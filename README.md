# Doctor Appointment Management System

## Introduction

The **Doctor Appointment Management System** is a web-based application designed to simplify and automate the process of booking and managing medical appointments in hospitals and clinics. The system provides an efficient platform for patients, doctors, and administrators to manage appointments, schedules, and medical records seamlessly. It replaces traditional manual appointment systems with a secure, reliable, and user-friendly digital solution.

The system reduces paperwork, minimizes scheduling conflicts, improves patient experience, and enhances overall hospital workflow efficiency.

---

## Key Features

### Admin Module

* User & Role Management (Patients, Doctors, Staff)
* Doctor Profile & Schedule Management
* Appointment Monitoring & Control
* Hospital & Department Management
* Reports & Analytics Generation
* Notifications & System Announcements

---

### Doctor Module

* Doctor Profile Management
* Availability & Schedule Configuration
* View Daily Appointments
* Appointment Approval / Rejection
* Patient Medical Record Access
* Consultation Fee Management

---

### Patient Module

* Patient Registration & Login
* Doctor Search (Specialization / Availability)
* Online Appointment Booking
* Appointment Rescheduling & Cancellation
* View Appointment Status
* Notification & Reminder Alerts

---

### Staff / Receptionist Module

* Appointment Coordination
* Patient Record Maintenance
* Billing & Fee Handling
* Report Assistance

---

## Highlights

* **Online Appointment Booking:** Patients can book appointments anytime without visiting the hospital.
* **Doctor Availability Management:** Doctors manage schedules and control appointment slots.
* **Real-Time Status Tracking:** Patients can track appointment confirmation and updates.
* **Admin Dashboard & Reports:** Centralized control with analytics and reporting.
* **Reduced Waiting Time:** Eliminates long queues and manual scheduling errors.
* **Secure & Reliable System:** Ensures data accuracy and controlled access.

---

## System Objectives

* To automate the manual doctor appointment process
* To reduce administrative workload and paperwork
* To provide patients with easy and quick appointment booking
* To improve doctor schedule management
* To maintain accurate patient and appointment records
* To enhance hospital service efficiency

---

## Technology Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript
* **Backend:** PHP
* **Database:** MySQL
* **Server:** XAMPP (Apache & MySQL)
* **Client-side Validation:** AJAX
* **Tools:** VS Code, phpMyAdmin, Browser Developer Tools
